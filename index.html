Personal Profile

Research Interests

I am currently interested in reinforcement learning and combinatorial optimization problems. I am also working on some classical machine learning and deep learning techniques for time series forecasting.

Previously, I worked on toric varieties, particularly on Eisenbud-Goto-type problems and spent most of my time on finding bounds for the kk-normality and Castelnuovo-Mumford regularity of projective toric varieties. My doctorate supervisor is Milena Hering.
