Personal Profile

Research Interests

I am currently interested in deep learning, in particular, natural language processing and computer vision. I am also working on some hybird models of classical and machine learning techniques for time series forecasting.

Previously, I worked on toric varieties, particularly on Eisenbud-Goto-type problems and spending most of my time now on finding bounds for the kk-normality and Castelnuovo-Mumford regularity of projective toric varieties. My doctorate supervisor is Milena Hering.
